MATERIAL

Our manufacturing staff works with cleanliness for your health, safety and quality standard for our products so our garments are safe and healthy. We are using 100% polyester fabric for our garments. Your safety is our concern.

BENEFITS TO ENVIRONMENT

It reduces the power and water consumption and Recycled polyester is almost the same as virgin polyester in terms of quality, but its production requires 59 percent less energy compared to virgin polyester so it's really beneficial to the environment.

FABRIC DETAILS

We are using 100% polyester material which is recycled from plastic such as plastic bottles. We use PET plastic for our material. The PET plastic is taken to the waste separation then cleaned, shredded and recycled into a new recycled polyester fiber. It reduces the consumption and gives new life to plastic waste. This polyester is strong and long lasting.

CARE

To preserve the same quality of this garment, we recommend you to read the instructions:

- Do not bleach

- Do not tumble dry

- Gentle dry clean

- Do not rub
